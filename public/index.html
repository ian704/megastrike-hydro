About Megastrike

Megastrike Hydro Drilling Solutions Ltd is a trusted water solutions company based in Kitale, Kenya. We specialize in delivering reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective groundwater development services across the country, particularly in Western and Northern regions.

Since our establishment in 2024, we have successfully handled projects of varying scale and complexity, supporting communities, institutions, and businesses with dependable water access solutions.

Our team is committed to professionalism, innovation, and environmental responsibility. We provide end-to-end services including consultancy, hydrogeological research, drilling, and system installation.

✦ Our Mission

To deliver efficient, sustainable, and high-quality water solutions that meet client needs.

✦ Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted and preferred borehole drilling and water solutions provider.

✦ Core Values

Integrity, professionalism, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

Hydrogeological Survey

Scientific assessment to determine groundwater availability and optimal drilling locations.

Geological Survey

Detailed study of soil and rock structures to support safe and effective drilling.

Hydrological Survey

Evaluation of water movement, distribution, and quality for sustainable usage.

Borehole Drilling & Casing

Professional drilling using modern equipment with durable casing installation.

Yield Testing

Accurate testing to determine borehole capacity and long-term performance.

Pump Installation

Installation of efficient water pumps tailored to your borehole system.

Water Tower Construction

Design and construction of elevated storage systems for reliable distribution.

Borehole Inspection

Maintenance and inspection services to ensure system efficiency and longevity.
