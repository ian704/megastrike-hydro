About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Water Solutions

Megastrike Hydro Drilling Solutions Ltd is a premier borehole drilling company based in Kitale, Western Kenya. Established in 2024, we have rapidly grown to become a trusted name in groundwater projects across Kenya.

We handle projects of all scales – from small domestic boreholes to large commercial installations – with the same commitment to excellence, professionalism, and integrity.

  • Licensed and certified drilling professionals
  • Modern drilling equipment and technology
  • Environmentally conscious practices
  • Transparent pricing and timelines
  • After-sales support and maintenance
